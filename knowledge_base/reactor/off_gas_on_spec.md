# Title
oxygen_offgas_on_spec.md
## Equipment
R-101 Oxidation reactor
## Normal operating range

Normal oxygen (O2) in offgas: between 3.0 % and 3.5%
Normal CO2 in offgass: between 2.0% and 2.5%

## Symptoms
- in normal conditions oxygen in offgasses concentration must stay within 3% and 3.5%
- oxygen concentration in offgass below 3% and above 3.5% is to be considered not normal

## Suggested action
Follow the approved operating procedure for high reactor temperature.

## Safety note
High oxygen in offgas must be treated as process safety relevant.
