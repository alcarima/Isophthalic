# Title
thick_indicator_not_ok.md
## Equipment
R-101 Oxidation Reactor
## Normal operating range
thickness index : 0.74 to 0.78
## Symptoms
- poor transfer from reactor to 1st crystallizer
- reactor level goes up

## Possible causes
- ratio from mixing vessel is not OK
- condenser_1 reflux is too low , need to check the deep pipe
- condenser_2 reflux ratio is too low, need to check the return lute
## Operator checks
1. check that the flowrate of para xylene, acetic acid , water are within specs
2. check that the condensate flow from condenser_1 is working OK
3. check steam pressure from condenser_1
4. check that the condensate flow from condenser_2 is working OK
5. check the steam pressure of condenser_2

## Suggested action
Follow the approved operating procedure for high reactor temperature.

Do not make large manual changes without supervisor confirmation.
## Safety note
Blockage of the flow from reactor to 1st condenser could lead ot reactor content overflow and possible blockage of the system
